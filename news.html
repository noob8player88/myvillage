News

Community Meeting Held

On 10th September, villagers gathered at the community center to discuss new water supply projects and local school renovation. Many suggestions were given and actions planned.

Health Camp Next Week

A free health checkup camp will be arranged by local volunteers in the main square on 25th September. All villagers are encouraged to attend for dental, vision, and general care.

New Farming Workshop

A workshop on modern farming techniques and sustainable agriculture will be organized by the agriculture department. Date: 2nd October.
