About

Our village is steeped in tradition. Founded over 200 years ago, it's a place where agriculture, culture, and community come together. From festivals to daily life, the people here are known for hospitality, craftsmanship, and strong bonds.

Languages spoken: Punjabi, Urdu. Main occupations: farming, livestock, handicrafts. Youth are increasingly using technology and gaming as well.
